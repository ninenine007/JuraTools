🧹 Text Utility

Text Sanitizer

Paste text from Word, Claude, PDFs, or any app — formatting is stripped instantly. Optionally convert straight quotes to curly quotes, then copy the clean text anywhere.

Paste here ⌘V or Ctrl+V — formatting is removed automatically
Options
Clean text
How it works:
  • Format stripping: reads only the plain-text version of your clipboard, discarding all HTML, RTF, and styling metadata.
  • Curly quotes: replaces straight " and ' with typographic quotes (“” ‘’) based on surrounding context. Apostrophes in contractions (don’t, it’s) are handled correctly.
