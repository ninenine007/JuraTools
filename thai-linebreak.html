Thai Legal Text Line Formatter

Automatically inserts line breaks into Thai text using proper Thai word segmentation.

This tool reformats long Thai text by inserting line breaks at appropriate positions without cutting words incorrectly. It is especially useful for legal drafts, contracts, regulations, and other formal documents that require controlled line length.

Line breaks are inserted based on Thai word boundaries (not character count alone).
