CCC Time & Deadline Calculator

Applies the time-counting rules of the Thai Civil and Commercial Code. Covers: the exclude-first-day rule with its business-opening exception (§193/3); calendar-based weeks, months, and years with the missing-day rule (§193/5); fractional-year periods converted to months then days (§193/6); period extensions (§193/7); and holiday/non-working-day shifts of the final day (§193/8). Also includes duration measurement between two dates (CCC vs. clear days) and sub-day time-span calculation under §193/2 — e.g., 09:56–10:30.

Extension & Holiday Shift §§193/7 · 193/8

Period Extension (§193/7)

When using the default start, the extension begins the day after the original deadline and that day is counted.

Non-working Days & Holiday Shift (§193/8)

Weeks are counted from Sunday as the first day, per the commentary under §193/5.
