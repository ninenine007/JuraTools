🔍 About This Site

This is a personal collection of tools to help myself and my friends work with text, legal documents, court judgments, and language-related tasks.

🧪 Available Tools

  • Legal Term Extractor – Extract law terms from Thai legal texts
  • PDF Concordance Generator – Generate keyword concordances
  • Thai-English Sentence Aligner – Help align translations
