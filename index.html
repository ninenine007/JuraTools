New Text Utility

Thai Text Line Formatter

Inserts clean line breaks into Thai text using proper Thai word boundaries (no mid-word splits). Useful for legal drafts, contracts, and regulations where controlled line length improves readability.

New Corporate / Voting

Shareholder Voting Simulator

Simulate voting outcomes across all allowed vote combinations per shareholder. Calculates “For” votes and checks pass/fail for majority (>50%), 2/3, and 3/4 thresholds.

New Corporate / Registers

Share Certificate Calculator

Manage shareholders, reorder the list, and generate certificate start/end ranges automatically based on share counts. Includes copy-to-clipboard for the certificate register table.

Open View source
New Math Utility

Percentage Calculators

Two simple tools for everyday % math. Percentage change: start value ± % = new value (solve any one). Basic %: “X is P% of Y” (solve X, P, or Y). Great for discounts, markups, VAT, and quick comparisons. Highlights the field it calculates.

New Legal / Dates

GM Notice & Registration (CCC s.1175)

Compute earliest GM or latest notice using 7/14 clear-days, enforce BOD → notice ordering, and get the 14-day registration deadline. Includes manual adjustments, printable timeline, and .ics export.
